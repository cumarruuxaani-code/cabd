Welcome to Dalag Agro Company

Dalag Agro Company is a modern agriculture company that produces high-quality crops and supports farmers with advanced farming techniques. We are committed to sustainable agriculture and food security in Somalia.

Dalag Agro Company was established to improve agricultural production and help farmers achieve better harvests. Our mission is to provide fresh farm products while protecting the environment.
